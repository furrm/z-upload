Mike Hotel and Apartments is located in the coastal village of Maleme , just 17km from Chania . The complex consists of an upper building , Mike's Hotel with the Restaurant and the Hotel Rooms and a lower building , Mike's Studios and Apartments with  the Studios -  Apartments , the Pool Bar and a uniquely shaped 174sqm Swimming Pool , with a children's section  .

The Hotel Rooms have been refurbished in 2011 with dark wood furniture with clean lines adding to their classic style and offer a variety of room categories.

The Studios and Apartments in the lower building , with 2 elevators , with modern design , have a fresh and airy feel to them and are best suited to those who wish to be near the Swimming Pool and Sea . All these units have a kitchenette and views either to the sea or the well-kept gardens .

Separating these two complexes is a stretch of 150m of cultivated land . The owners  Organically Grow their own produce and use this in their restaurant .
Surrounded by palm trees and well-kept gardens, the Pool Bar and Swimming Pool area , just steps from Maleme's pebble Beach with lifeguard , are the ideal place at which you can relax , sunbathe and enjoy a refreshment .

Near  you will also find Beach Volley , Mini soccer , Tennis Court ,  Supermarket , Mini market , Car Rental , ATM , pharmacy , Doctor's Office , Bakery, Foreign Press Store , Restaurants , Bars and Cafes.

Mike Hotel and Apartments Chania is an ideal base from which to explore west Crete, its famed Beaches and archaeological historical sites from a short drive to Chania Hotel center and its old Venetian town makes for a wonderful visit . This is just the place to be if you are looking to relax , rejuvenate and enjoy sun-blessed Crete  !
